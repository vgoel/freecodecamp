Timeline of Admiral Stockdale's Life

  • Dec 23, 1923 - Born in Abingdon, Illinois, U.S.
  • 1943 - Entered US Naval Academy
  • 1946 - Graduates Naval Academy
  • 1950 - Graduates as a Naval Aviator
  • 1959 - Enters Stanford University to study International Relations
  • 9 September 1965 - Stockdale's A-4 Skyhawk shot down in a mission over North Vietnam. He is captured as a POW
  • February 12, 1973 - Released as a POW
  • 1992 - Vice Presidential Candidate
  • July 5, 2005 - Died 88 years old in Cornonado, California
